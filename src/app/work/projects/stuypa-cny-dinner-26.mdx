# Overview

A 3-hour community dinner celebrating the Year of the Horse, capturing guests and key moments throughout the evening.

## Task & Approach

Document the full event from start to finish, combining candid and posed shots to capture atmosphere and meaningful interactions. Focused on framing wide shots for context and close-ups to highlight details and emotions.

## Equipment 

Full-frame Sony hybrid camera with a versatile lens, enabling both wide and close-up shots while adapting to low-light conditions and dynamic movement.

## Obstacles & Solutions

- **Low-light environment** → Adjusted ISO and aperture to maintain clarity while minimizing noise.
- **Variable lighting** → Applied careful post-processing to create a consistent, natural look across the gallery.
- **Flickering electronic displays** → Managed by turning displays off for key shots when possible.

## Outcome

Edited and delivered a complete gallery for organizers’ use, including social media-ready images, successfully capturing the event’s atmosphere and key moments.
